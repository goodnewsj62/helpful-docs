google search tip


wrap text in quotes to find exact atch: 

--"find this"

find on a particular site with the sight tag, only what is needed is the site url and other site parameter like so:

-- site:github.com my-project

Use the dash to exculde word from search 

-- good guns - ak47


Use the before, after and two dots value range to make a search

-- good guns after 2006

--good guns before 2010

-- good guns 2010..2022

Use the filetype to look for a filetype you're looking for

-- filetype:pdf types of horses


to know if google has cached your website

--cache:mywebsite.com

use the related tag to find related website 

-- related:x.com
